# Optron AI Website — Harness Placement Specification

**Status:** Approved public messaging specification  
**Date:** 2026-07-17

## Final recommendation

Do not replace the hero headline with “Optron AI Harness.” The hero must continue to sell the product category users can understand:

> **The World’s First Consumer AI Autopilot™**

Place the Harness immediately around the hero in a secondary but clearly visible position.

## Recommended hero structure

### Small premium badge or eyebrow above the headline

> **Powered by the Optron AI Harness™**

### Main headline

> **The World’s First Consumer AI Autopilot™**

### Subheading

> Optron coordinates the right models, tools, memory, connectors, agents, and workflows for each mission — through the Optron AI Harness™.

### Optional transition line

> **From Gen AI to AI Autopilot™**

## Visual priority

1. Optron AI logo and identity
2. The World’s First Consumer AI Autopilot™
3. Powered by the Optron AI Harness™
4. Short explanation and call to action

The Harness line should be visually distinct, but smaller than the principal hero claim. Recommended treatments include a compact pill, eyebrow label, thin bordered badge, or short bold line directly above or below the headline.

## Dedicated homepage section

Add a section after the first product-benefit or capability block:

### Meet the Optron AI Harness™

> The orchestration architecture that coordinates models, tools, memory, connectors, agents, policy, and execution into one AI Autopilot mission.

Use three concise explanatory blocks:

- **Coordinate intelligence:** select and combine the right models and sources.
- **Carry mission context:** align memory, projects, tools, and live knowledge.
- **Move toward execution:** coordinate agents, connectors, workflows, recovery, and future actions.

Closing line:

> **A router chooses. A harness coordinates.**

## About-page placement

Create a subsection named:

> **What is the Optron AI Harness™?**

Explain the distinction:

- Optron AI is the product.
- Consumer AI Autopilot™ is the category.
- Optron AI Harness™ is the proprietary architecture beneath it.

## Messaging guardrails

- Do not make “Harness” larger than the product name or principal category claim.
- Do not describe Optron as merely a router or model aggregator.
- Do not overload the hero with a technical architecture diagram.
- Do not hide Harness only in technical documentation; it must appear on the homepage.
- Use ™ on the first prominent occurrence per page; repeated occurrences may omit the symbol for visual cleanliness.
