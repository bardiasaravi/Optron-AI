## Locked public identity

> **Optron AI — The World’s First Consumer AI Autopilot™**

Optron AI is not positioned as another chatbot, single model, search engine, or simple multi-model router. Its public category is **Consumer AI Autopilot™**.

## Locked architecture identity

> **Optron AI Harness™**

The Optron AI Harness™ is the proprietary mission-orchestration architecture beneath the product. It coordinates models, tools, sources, memory, connectors, agents, assistants, policy, live knowledge, workflow state, recovery, and execution into one consumer-facing AI Autopilot experience.

A router selects a path. An aggregator gathers options. The Optron AI Harness™ coordinates the full mission.

## Website messaging rule

The website must preserve both layers:

- The hero must lead with the user-facing category: **The World’s First Consumer AI Autopilot™**.
- The Harness must appear directly beneath the main hero claim as a prominent supporting architecture line: **Powered by the Optron AI Harness™**.
- The Harness must also have a dedicated explanatory section lower on the homepage or About page.
- The word “Harness” must never replace “Consumer AI Autopilot” as the primary product category.
