## Purpose

This addendum formally records two major extensions to the Optron AI Big Book:

1. the addition of the **Optron AI Harness™** as the core orchestration architecture beneath Optron AI; and
2. the addition of the **Optron Mission Room™** as the shared human–AI mission environment.

These additions do not replace Optron AI’s public category. They complete the architecture and product hierarchy around it.

## Optron AI Harness™

The Optron AI Harness™ is the governed mission-orchestration architecture that coordinates the full mission lifecycle rather than merely selecting a model.

It may coordinate:

- model selection and multi-model collaboration;
- tools, connectors, sources, live knowledge, and retrieval;
- memory, context, identity, policy, and permissions;
- mission planning, delegation, execution, verification, fallback, and recovery;
- agents, advisors, specialists, automation, and human approval points;
- continuing mission state across sessions, files, tools, and participants.

A router chooses a path. An aggregator presents options. The Optron AI Harness™ coordinates the mission.

### Architectural relationship

> **Consumer AI Autopilot™ is what the user experiences. Optron AI Harness™ is how Optron coordinates the mission.**

The Harness must never replace Consumer AI Autopilot™ as the primary outward-facing product category.

## Optron Mission Room™

The Optron Mission Room™ is the persistent shared environment where people and AI work together around one continuing mission.

It is not merely a chat room, project board, or messaging surface. It is the collaborative operating layer of the Consumer AI Autopilot™.

The Mission Room may support:

- shared goals and mission state;
- human owners, advisors, specialists, and AI participants;
- connected conversations, files, evidence, decisions, tasks, and approvals;
- visible roles, ownership, delegation, and handoffs;
- asynchronous continuation and preserved progress;
- review, verification, escalation, recovery, and auditability;
- scheduled, recurring, follow-up, monitoring, and condition-based automation;
- continuity across people, models, agents, connectors, and tools.

### Mission Room principle

> **Mission Room is where people and AI work together around the mission. Harness is the system that coordinates how the mission moves. Autopilot is the experience delivered to the user.**

## Coworking model

Optron coworking means humans and AI participate inside one connected mission context rather than exchanging isolated prompts.

The system should preserve:

- who is responsible;
- what has been decided;
- what evidence supports the decision;
- what remains unresolved;
- what action is next;
- what requires permission or review;
- what can continue automatically;
- and how the mission can recover when a participant, model, connector, or tool fails.

## Automation model

Automation inside Optron must remain:

- permission-aware;
- transparent;
- interruptible;
- reviewable;
- recoverable;
- connected to the relevant mission state;
- and bounded by policy, identity, safety, and user control.

Automation is not uncontrolled autonomy. It is governed continuation of an approved mission.

## Core system relationship

- Head Office;
