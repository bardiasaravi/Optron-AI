## Coworking model

Optron coworking means humans and AI participate inside one connected mission context rather than exchanging isolated prompts.

The system should preserve:

- who is responsible;
- what has been decided;
- what evidence supports the decision;
- what remains unresolved;
- what action is next;
- what requires permission or review;
- what can continue automatically;
- and how the mission can recover when a participant, model, connector, or tool fails.

## Automation model

Automation inside Optron must remain:

- permission-aware;
- transparent;
- interruptible;
- reviewable;
- recoverable;
- connected to the relevant mission state;
- and bounded by policy, identity, safety, and user control.

Automation is not uncontrolled autonomy. It is governed continuation of an approved mission.
