# Optron AI Big Book Amendment R2 — Mission Room, Harness, Coworking, and Automation

**Date:** 2026-07-17  
**Status:** ACTIVE PUBLIC AMENDMENT  
**Applies to:** Optron AI Big Book — Canonical Public Edition R2

## Amendment purpose

This amendment formally adds the Optron Mission Room™ and clarifies the relationship between the user-facing Consumer AI Autopilot™, the Optron AI Harness™, coworking, and automation.

## Canonical additions

### Optron AI Harness™

The Optron AI Harness™ is the umbrella orchestration architecture behind Optron AI. It coordinates models, context, memory, tools, connectors, agents, workflows, execution, recovery, freshness, and governance.

It is not merely a router. Routing is one capability within the larger Harness system.

### Optron Mission Room™

The Optron Mission Room™ is the collaborative operating environment in which people and AI work around a shared mission.

It may include:

- shared mission goals and state;
- human and AI participants;
- advisor, specialist, agent, and decision roles;
- files, evidence, constraints, and decisions;
- task ownership and progress;
- review and approval checkpoints;
- recurring, scheduled, and conditional automation;
- activity history and resumable context.

### Coworking model

Coworking means that humans and AI do not operate as isolated prompt-and-response endpoints. They work inside one continuing mission context, with visible roles, shared progress, and controlled handoffs.

### Automation model

Automation extends the mission beyond a single chat session. Approved actions may run on a schedule, recur, or respond to conditions while remaining attached to the mission’s context, permissions, and audit trail.

Automation must be:

- permission-aware;
- transparent;
- interruptible;
- reviewable;
- recoverable;
- aligned with the user’s stated goal.

## Locked relationship

- **Consumer AI Autopilot™** is the category and experience.
- **Optron AI Harness™** is the orchestration architecture.
- **Optron Mission Room™** is the shared collaborative operating surface.
- **Automation** is a governed capability of the mission system.

## Documentation rule

All future public Optron documentation should preserve this relationship and use the exact trademark forms listed above. This amendment does not disclose private implementation details, security controls, or confidential architecture.
